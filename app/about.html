Jen's Coaching Journey

  • Why I Became a Coach
  • Background
  • Credentials
I was once in your shoes. I was a CEO who felt stuck, frusrated and confused. I loved my company, but I felt like I was too involved in the day-to-day to grow..............blah blah,,,,, Trust me, there’s a way out.

I know because I’ve been there. I found freedom and so can you. For me, freedom meant growing my company to the point where I was able to sell and retire. For you, it may be something entirely different. Whatever your Freedom Goal(TM), you can get there.

How did I do it? Not on my own, that’s for sure. I knew where I wanted to be, and I recognized that I wasn’t going to get there if I kept doing things the same way I had been. So I asked for help. I hired a coach.

Through coaching, I discovered what was holding me back, learned how to align my team towards a common goal, and marched towards that goal with laser-like focus. Not only that, it improved my quality of life. X years later, I’m still so blown away by the difference a coach made for me, I simply had to pay it forward and become one myself.

Now, I work with CEOs who need guidance to take their business to the next level. Using the principles and tools of the Rockefeller Habits, I coach CEOs through the process of defining their Freedom Goal, implementing a framework to achieve it, and getting their team aligned and moving towards the same goal. Through awareness, discipline and accountability, you too can find freedom from what’s holding your business back from growth. Trust me, it will change your life.
