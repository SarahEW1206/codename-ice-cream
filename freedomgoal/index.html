FREEDOM GOAL™ (n)

A specific objective that you have for your business, which, when reached, will give you a sense of freedom and an improved quality of life.

Upon reaching your Freedom Goal, you'll be able to say: "This is where I want to be."

Examples include: no longer managing day-to-day tasks, making key hires, increased sales growth, selling your company, retiring, etc.
